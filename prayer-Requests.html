Submit your prayer requests and let the power of prayer work in your life

"Therefore, confess your sins to one another and pray for one another, that you may be healed. The prayer of a righteous person has great power as it is working." - James 5:16
We understand that life can be challenging and we want to be a source of support and encouragement for you. That is why we have created this prayer request page on our website. This page is a way for you to share your needs and concerns with the church community and to know that you are being lifted up in prayer.

Our intercessory team will be praying for your requests on a regular basis. You can also share updates on how your requests have been answered, so that we can give praise to God for His faithfulness. We believe in the power of prayer and we want to stand with you as you navigate through life's challenges.

We believe that the Bible teaches us that prayer is an essential part of the Christian life and it is a powerful tool for us to use to connect with God and to receive His guidance and support. We encourage you to take the time to submit your requests and allow the power of prayer to work in your life. We are here for you and we want to support you in your journey.
