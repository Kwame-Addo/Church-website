Vision and Mision

What We Stand For

Our Mandate

As a church, it is our mandate to spread the message of Jesus, show love and compassion as written in 1 John 4:7-8 "God is love", and welcome all to join us in worship and spiritual growth.

Our Vision

Our vision as a church: To expand and share the gospel, showing love as stated in John 13:35 "By this all will know you are my disciples."

Mission Statement

Our mission as a Ghanaian church: to make disciples and empower believers, as stated in Matt 28:19 "Make disciples of all nations" by sharing the gospel, offering fellowship and service opportunities, and teaching the Bible.

Beliefs

What We Believe

The Trinity

As a Ghanaian church, we believe in the doctrine of the Trinity: One God in three persons - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - fully God, distinct and united. As stated in Matt 3:16-17, it is essential to our understanding of God and foundational to our faith.

The Separation

As a Ghanaian church, we believe in living a life separated from sin, as stated in 2 Cor 7:1 "Let us purify ourselves." Through faith in Jesus Christ, His death and resurrection, we are reconciled to God, turning away from our sins and seeking guidance of the Holy Spirit to live a holy and godly life.

The Church

As a Ghanaian church, we believe in the church as the body of Christ, united in faith and following Jesus as stated in Ephesians 4:12 "to prepare God's people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up." A place of worship, fellowship, spiritual growth and service.
