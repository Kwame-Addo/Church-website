Apostle and Lady Rev

Our Founders

The founders of RVFC, a growing church in the Ga East Municipal District - Kwabenya, are Apostle Solomon Oduro and his wife Lady Reverend Charlotte Oduro. They have been in the ministry for more than 20 years, and they are passionate about showing the love of Jesus to everyone they come in contact with. With a variety of ministries and programs that address the spiritual and practical needs of attendees, RVFC has developed under their direction into a haven of hope and healing for many people in the neighborhood. Through their ministry in Kwabenya, the pair, who are renowned for their kindness, compassion, and dedication to serving people, have impacted many lives.

More about the Church

About RVFC

This ministry began in the year of 2002 with a dozen people meeting in homes and ministering throughout Kwabenya. Led by the call of God to preach the gospel, Apostle Solomon Oduro and his wife Lady Reverend Charlotte Oduro founded Royal Victory Family Church International(RVFCI).
RVFCI has continued to fulfill the assignment of God. We are commited to helping people find new life in Christ, learn the power of the word of God, experience God's power, and understand the principles of faith.
