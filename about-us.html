Our Founders

Discover a place where faith and community come together at RVFC. More about the Church

About RVFC

RVFCI is a church that focuses on spreading the love of Jesus through various programs and support groups. We provide guidance, support and a community of believers dedicated to helping others grow in their faith. Our goal is to empower individuals to live out their faith and make a positive impact in their community. Join us for worship, fellowship and to experience the power of faith in action.

Adult Ministry

Our Adult Ministry offers guidance and support for individuals to deepen their faith and make a positive impact in the community.

Youth Ministry

Our Youth Ministry provides a supportive community for young people to explore and grow in their faith.

Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry offers age-appropriate activities and teaching to help children learn about and grow in their faith.

Services

Meeting Hours

Our Sunday morning service at RVFCI is a time for the community to come together and worship the Lord. The service starts promptly at 9am and goes until 12pm. During the service, we sing lively gospel songs and hear powerful sermons delivered by our pastors. The sermons are uplifting, insightful and practical, meant to inspire and encourage the attendees to live out their faith in their daily lives. The service is filled with the presence of God, and it's a perfect time to connect with the community, grow in your faith and experience the love of Jesus in action.

Our Wednesday morning service, also known as "Court Hour Prayers," is a time for the community to come together and deepen their connection with the Lord through intense prayer. The service starts at 6am and goes until 10am. During this time, we spend time in worship and listen to teachings on the power of prayer, and then we engage in individual and corporate prayer. Attendees are encouraged to bring their specific prayer requests, as the service is a powerful time for spiritual breakthroughs, where people can experience the love, mercy and power of God. The service is also a time for spiritual growth and strengthening of one's faith, as well as a time for fellowship and building a deeper sense of community.

Our Friday All-Night Service, held on the last Friday of every month, is a time for the community to come together and deepen their spiritual connection with God in an extended and meaningful way. The service starts at 8pm and goes until 4am the next day. During this time, we sing gospel songs, hear inspiring sermons and engage in a time of focused and intense prayers for specific needs and concerns. The service is designed to be a powerful and transformative experience, where people can come before God with their burdens and leave with renewed hope and faith. The service is also a time for fellowship and building a deeper sense of community as we come together in worship, united in our pursuit of a deeper relationship with God. It's an opportunity to spend an extended time in worship and connect with the Lord in a deeper way while leaving with a sense of hope and faith.
Church Premises

Where we worship

"Let the word of Christ dwell in you richly in all wisdom, teaching and admonishing one another in psalms and hymns and spiritual songs, singing with grace in your hearts to the Lord." - Colossians 3:16 (NKJV)

Our place of worship at RVFCI is located in the heart of Kwabenya, opposite the Puma Filling Station in Accra. Our facility is designed to provide an environment that is conducive to worship and fellowship. We have a spacious sanctuary that can comfortably seat hundreds of people, equipped with state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems to enhance the worship experience. Our sanctuary is a place of beauty and inspiration, where people can come together to lift their voices in praise to the Lord. Our facility also includes classrooms, offices and other spaces where our various programs and support groups are held. Our church is a welcoming and inclusive community, where everyone is welcome to come and experience the love of Jesus. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a long-time member, you'll find a warm and friendly community at RVFCI. Our facility is easily accessible by car and public transportation and we have ample parking space for our attendees. Come and join us for worship, fellowship and experience the love of Jesus in action.

Remember it is not just a building, it's a sanctuary where people can come to connect with God, find hope and experience the love of Jesus. It's a place where people can come to be transformed by the power of the Holy Spirit, and leave feeling uplifted and encouraged. The atmosphere of our sanctuary is peaceful and serene, making it an ideal place for introspection, contemplation and for having personal encounters with God. Our dedicated team of leaders and volunteers work hard to ensure that every aspect of our church services and programs are designed to bring glory to God and to be a blessing to our community. We invite you to come and be a part of our church family and experience the presence of God in our place of worship.

Where We are headed

At RVFCI, we believe that we are called to be a light in our community and to make a positive impact in the lives of those around us. We are constantly seeking ways to grow and reach more people with the love and message of Jesus Christ. As we look to the future, we are excited about the opportunities ahead of us to expand our reach, both locally and globally.
We are committed to being a church that is known for its love, compassion, and generosity. We believe that the love of Jesus can change lives, and we are passionate about sharing that love with those around us. We strive to be a church where people can find hope, healing and acceptance.
We are also focused on equipping our members to become effective ambassadors for Christ in their daily lives, by providing training and resources that will help them to share their faith in a meaningful and impactful way. We believe that every believer has a unique role to play in advancing the kingdom of God, and we are committed to helping them discover and fulfill that role.
In the coming years, we plan to expand our outreach efforts to include more mission trips, community service projects and other opportunities to serve the needs of our community. We are also looking to expand our facility to better serve our growing congregation and the needs of our community. We are committed to being a church that is reaching out and making a difference in the world.

Fellowships and Ministries

Church Groups

At RVFCI, we believe that peace is vital for a healthy and thriving community. Our peace fellowship is dedicated to promoting peace and understanding among our members and the community as a whole. We hold regular meetings and events aimed at fostering a culture of peace, love and understanding. The fellowship also provides support and guidance for members who may be going through difficult times and in need of peace and healing. We believe that through prayer, support, and guidance, we can create a more peaceful community.

Love is at the core of our faith and it is what drives us at RVFCI. Our love fellowship is dedicated to promoting love and unity among our members and the community. We hold regular meetings and events aimed at fostering a culture of love and acceptance. The fellowship also provides support and guidance for members who may be going through difficult times and in need of love and healing. We believe that through love and support, we can create a more loving and accepting community.

Unity is essential for a thriving church community. Our unity fellowship is dedicated to promoting unity and togetherness among our members and the community. We hold regular meetings and events aimed at fostering a culture of unity and understanding. The fellowship also provides support and guidance for members who may be going through difficult times and in need of unity and healing. We believe that through unity, support, and guidance, we can create a more united and harmonious community.

RVFCI's choir is a group of dedicated members who lead the congregation in worship through music. They perform a variety of gospel and contemporary songs, chosen to fit the theme of the service. The choir is led by a team of musical directors and is committed to using their talents to bring glory to God and to be a blessing to the congregation. They are open to new members who are passionate about music and worship. They add to the worship service by creating an atmosphere of worship and lead the congregation in singing praises to God.

RVFCI's Prayer Tower is a dedicated group of members who meet regularly to pray for the church, community and the world. Led by trained intercessors, the group is open to new members who are passionate about prayer. They serve as an essential part of the church's spiritual foundation, providing support and guidance through prayer and intercession.

The RVFCI Ushering Team welcomes guests, directs seating, and assists during services, creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for all attendees. Led by experienced leaders, the team is open to new members who want to serve and help create a comfortable environment for all attendees.
